Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within the US takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available for an additional charge. International shipping takes 10-15 business days.

Are the toys safe for children?

Yes! Our toys are made from natural wood and painted with non-toxic vegetable dyes and enamel paints. However, small parts may pose choking hazards for children under 3.

Do you accept bulk orders?

Yes, we welcome bulk orders for events, corporate gifts, and retailers. Contact us for special pricing and custom requirements.
